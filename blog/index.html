Impact of Sports Biopics on Indian Society
Sports biopics in India, such as *"Dangal"* and *"Mary Kom,"* inspire future generations, promote lesser-known sports, challenge gender norms, and highlight mental toughness. They foster national pride and encourage social and policy changes, leading to greater support for athletes. These films significantly impact Indian society, shaping perceptions and driving progress.

How to Prevent Injuries While Playing Sports
Preventing sports injuries involves proper warm-up and cool-down, using appropriate gear, focusing on technique, building strength and flexibility, staying hydrated, and listening to your body. Gradually increase intensity, get adequate rest, and have regular check-ups. A proactive approach to these practices helps minimize the risk of injury and enhances performance.
